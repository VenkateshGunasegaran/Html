1.Gautam Adani

Gautam Shantilal Adani is an Indian billionaire industrialist who is the founder and chairman of Adani Group, a multinational conglomerate involved in port development and operations in India.

2.Mukesh Ambani

Mukesh Dhirubhai Ambani is an Indian billionaire businessman. He is currently the chairman and managing director of Reliance Industries, India's most valuable company by market value

3.Shiv Nadar

Shiv Nadar is an Indian billionaire industrialist and philanthropist. He is the founder and chairman emeritus of HCL Technologies and the Shiv Nadar Foundation.

4.Anil Ambani

Anil Dhirubhai Ambani is an Indian business man chairman and managing director of Reliance Group. The Reliance Group was created in July 2006 following a demerger from Reliance Industries Limited.

5.Ritesh Agarwal

Ritesh Agarwal is an Indian entrepreneur who is the founder and CEO of OYO Rooms
